Dec 20–22

Athens

The heart of classical Greece—home to the Acropolis and cradle of democracy. Compact, walkable, and full of cozy tavernas.

  • Acropolis & Parthenon; Acropolis Museum
  • Ancient Agora & Plaka lanes
  • Optional sunset at Cape Sounion (Temple of Poseidon)
HistoryKid‑friendlyVegetarian options
Dec 23

Meteora Overnight

Otherworldly monasteries perched on sandstone pillars. Stay overnight to catch the golden-hour views.

  • Drive Athens → Kalambaka (~4.5h) via Thermopylae stop
  • Sunset viewpoint on arrival; 2–3 monasteries next morning
  • Stay in Kastraki/Kalambaka for easy access
Scenic driveLight hikesPhotography
Dec 24

Santorini (1 Night)

Volcanic caldera vistas, blue domes, and tranquil winter streets—perfect for a swift highlight stop.

  • Short flight ATH → JTR (~50m)
  • Fira–Oia stroll; Oia sunset
  • Akrotiri site if time allows
Iconic viewsQuiet winterPhoto stop
Dec 25–27

Rome

The Eternal City: layers of empire, art, and street life. Festive lights and markets in late December.

  • Colosseum & Roman Forum (prebook)
  • Vatican Museums & St. Peter’s
  • Trastevere evening walk
HistoryFestiveGreat gelato
Dec 28–29

Naples & Pompeii

Lively historic center and gateway to Pompeii—one of the world’s most immersive archaeological sites.

  • High‑speed train Rome → Naples (~1h 10m)
  • Pompeii day (guided or DIY via Circumvesuviana)
  • Vegetarian pizza‑making class

Florence Base

Renaissance art and riverfront charm—an ideal base for Pisa and a Venice day trip.

  • Duomo, Uffizi, Ponte Vecchio
  • Pisa day trip (~1h each way)
  • Venice day trip (~2h each way)
